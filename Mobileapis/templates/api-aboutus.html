Inspired by the emerging trend of shared economy and crowd sourcing, AIRDELY TECHNOLOGIES PTE LTD (AIRDELY) is established to build on the crowd shipping concept, leveraging on peer-to-peer delivery by providing a platform to match air courier needs to the social activity of air traveling.

AIRDELY aspires to revolutionise the courier industry by providing a mobile application and website for booking and registration for both customer (parcel sender) and air traveler supported by automated task assignment matching software to deliver parcel fast and cheap and at the same time for the air traveller to earn money whenever they fly. AIRDELY is revolutionising the travel and courier industry, making overseas travel inexpensive and fun to travel, turning a cost centre into a potential earning and profit centre. Air traveller will be able to monetise their unused luggage allowances by carrying parcels for AIRDELY’s customers.

AIRDELY Centre will provide administrative and parcel security screening to ensure that all parcels are safe to deliver by our air traveller. The parcels will then be handed over to the Air Traveller to carry it to the overseas airport for delivery to the Customer Recipient’s address. In addition, all parcels transacted via AIRDELY platform will be insured against damages and losses. Ultimately, AIRDELY hopes to make travelling fun and inexpensive.

All air travellers must be vetted through our screening system to ensure their trust and dedication to handle and deliver parcels for our customers to their destination airport.

AIRDELY is the first mover using this concept in the courier industry and we aim to be the best in the industry. AIRDELY aims to be a global, multi-nation, multi-cities company, with collection centres in most airports and deliveries to all areas globally.

  • AIRDELY can achieve a much faster delivery timeline at a lower cost even for last minute delivery then most of the bigger and more established courier companies. By leveraging on the travellers’ destinations, it opens up the number of destinations available for delivery. Personal care and handling of the parcels by the air traveller himself.
  • AIRDELY will help the air travellers to optimise his unused luggage allowances to carry parcels meant for their destined airport for a given fee, according to the weight and value of the parcels which they are carrying. Thus, creating an all-win situation for all parties, the air travellers can earn extra allowances even though on a social activity, the customer is able to deliver his parcel at a shorter time frame and at a lower cost.

Currently, AIRDELY Apps is only available for parcels outbound from Singapore. Whenever you have a parcel to send, call AIRDELY. Whenever you are travelling overseas, call AIRDELY. Always remember AIRDELY – Fly and Earn. Save to Deliver.
